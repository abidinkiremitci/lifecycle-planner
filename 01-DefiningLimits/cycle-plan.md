# 01-DefiningLimits – Cycle Plan

## Overview

- **Focus:** Re-establish baseline fitness, progressively push to current capacity limits, and finish with a testing week to define working maxes and conditioning benchmarks for 2026.
- **Duration:** 4 weeks (2026-03-16 to 2026-04-12)
- **Days per week:** 5
  - Mon / Wed / Fri: Functional fitness (strength + metcon + skill/core)
  - Tue: Indoor rowing (30–60 min, goal: 10 km under 50 min)
  - Thu: Running (30–60 min, goal: 10 km under 50 min)

### Goals
- Find current 3–5 RM working loads for Front Squat, Deadlift, and Back Squat.
- Re-calibrate metcon pacing and sustainable cardio capacity.
- Lock in consistent HSPU progressions — target wall-walk-to-freestanding-hold by Week 4.
- Rebuild double-under consistency from singles/attempts (Week 1) to sets of 30+ (Week 4).
- **Rowing:** Build aerobic base toward 10 km under 50 min (target split ≤2:30/500 m).
- **Running:** Build aerobic base toward 10 km under 50 min (target pace ~5:00 min/km).

## Nutrition

### Cycle Nutrition Goals
- Support re-activation through conservative fuelling in Week 1 — no excess, no deficit.
- Incrementally increase carbs alongside load increases in Weeks 2–3 to match training demand.
- Peak fuelling in Week 4 — glycogen saturation is the primary lever for 1RM and time-trial performance.
- Protein stays fixed at 185 g/day throughout — recovery anchor across all phases.

### Cycle Phase Adjustments

| Phase | Week | Calorie Adjustment | Carb Adjustment | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Deload / Reactivation | Week 1 | -5% from targets | -40 to -50 g on FF days | Low volume — no need to oversupply; protect protein |
| Build | Week 2 | At target baseline | As per baseline targets | Match calorie/carb increase to load step-up |
| Push | Week 3 | +5% on FF days | +50 to +80 g on FF days | High-output week — do not under-fuel; endurance/rest unchanged |
| Test | Week 4 | +5 to +8% on all training days | Carb-load 24-36 h before each max-effort day | Glycogen saturation before 1RM tests and time-trials |
