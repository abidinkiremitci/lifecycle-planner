# Week 1 – Deload / Reactivation (2026-03-16 to 2026-03-22)

## Weekly Overview
- Cycle: 01-DefiningLimits
- Week: 1
- Dates: 2026-03-16 to 2026-03-22
- Load target: ~55%
- Metcon target: 15–18 min
- Focus: Re-establish movement patterns and baseline conditioning; conservative loads, simple movements, no complex barbell cycling
- Days: Monday (FF), Tuesday (Row), Wednesday (FF), Thursday (Run), Friday (FF)

## Notes
- First week of the cycle — priority is quality of movement, not intensity.
- Use this week to find comfortable working loads; note them for Week 2 progression.
- Metcons are intentionally short and simple; stay aerobic and don’t redline.
- Rowing: easy zone 2 base — building toward 10 km sub-50 min (target by Week 4).
- Running: easy zone 2 base — building toward 10 km sub-50 min (target by Week 4).
- One cardio movement per functional fitness metcon only.
- No kipping pull-ups, toes-to-bar, or chest-to-bar.
- No bench press, strict press, or back squats in metcons.
- Space: 5 m × 4 m — short shuttles only, no continuous carries > 20 m.

## Monday — Front Squat (Functional Fitness)

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 60s easy row
  - 8 air squats with 3 s pause at bottom
  - 10 scapular pull-ups + 6 PVC pass-throughs
  - 8 glute bridges + 8 hip circles/side

### Strength (15 min)
- Front Squat 3×5 @ ~55%
  - Tempo: 3-1-1 (3 s down, 1 s pause in hole, 1 s up)
  - Cues: elbows up, upright torso, full depth with control
  - Rest: 90 s between sets
- Optional finish: 2 × 8 goblet squat with empty pause (bodyweight or light KB) for positional feel

### Metcon (15 min AMRAP)
- 250 m row — sole cardio modality
  - Alt (no rower): 75 single-unders or 30 DU attempts
- 10 goblet squats (16 kg KB)
- 8 ring rows (substitute: 8 strict pull-ups or 10 resistance-band pull-aparts if ring rows too easy)
- Target: 4–5 rounds; keep row consistent, move steadily, no sprinting

### Skill / Core (12 min)
- HSPU progression (6 min): 4 × wall walk to 10–15 s nose-to-wall hold; rest 45 s between sets
- Core (6 min): 3 rounds × (15 hollow rocks + 20 s plank hold)

### Cool-down (10 min)
- Couch stretch 2 × 45 s/side
- Seated single-leg hamstring fold 2 × 45 s/side
- Thoracic extension over foam roller 1 × 60 s
- Box breathing 4-2-6 × 2 min

### Score
- Strength: Front Squat working weight — 50 kg
- Metcon: 6 rounds + 50m reps
- Notes: front squat felt difficult but i could fond my pace for metcon to complete nicely.

## Tuesday — Indoor Rowing

### Warm-up (5 min)
- 3 min easy row (rate 18 spm, focus on long stroke and full recovery)
- 8 hip flexor rocks/side + 8 shoulder rolls + 10 thoracic twists seated on erg

### Main Row (25 min steady-state)
- **4,000 m** at easy aerobic pace
  - Target split: ≤2:50/500 m (RPE 5–6, conversational pace, nasal breathing preferred)
  - Stroke rate: 18–20 spm
  - Focus: long stroke, full leg drive, relaxed arms — technique over pace today
  - If 4 km feels genuinely easy, add 500 m; if breathing is laboured, back off to 3,000 m

### Cool-down (5 min)
- 2 min easy row (rate 16 spm)
- Wall calf stretch 2 × 30 s/side
- Seated hip opener (figure-four on erg) 30 s/side

## Wednesday — Deadlift (Functional Fitness)

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 60 s easy row
  - 10 glute bridges + 10 bodyweight Romanian deadlifts
  - 8 hip hinge with PVC + 6 scapular push-ups
  - 8 leg swings/side + 6 deep reverse lunges/side

### Strength (15 min)
- Deadlift 3×5 @ ~55%
  - Cues: hips back to the bar, neutral spine throughout, controlled descent (3 s down)
  - Rest: 2 min between sets
- Accessory: 2 × 8 single-leg RDL (bodyweight or light DB) for hip stability

### Metcon (15 min AMRAP)
- 15 box step-ups (alternating legs, bodyweight) — sole cardio modality
  - Alt: 45 single-unders or 15 DU attempts
- 10 KB swings (16 kg, Russian — eye level)
- 8 push-ups (full, or knee if needed)
- Target: 5–6 rounds; steady pace, maintain hip hinge on swings

### Skill / Core (10 min)
- HSPU progression (5 min): 3 × 5 pike push-ups from box or bench (elevated feet)
- Core (5 min): 3 rounds × (10 dead bugs/side + 20 s side plank/side)

### Cool-down (10 min)
- Prone quad stretch 2 × 45 s/side
- Lat band stretch 2 × 45 s/side
- Kneeling hip flexor lunge stretch 2 × 45 s/side
- Supine 90/90 diaphragmatic breathing × 2 min

### Score
- Strength: Deadlift working weight — 100 kg
- Metcon: 8 rounds + 0 reps
- Notes: I found a pace to do 8r, there were 90 seconds but i stopped not to push myself in the first week.

## Thursday — Running

### Warm-up (5 min)
- 3 min easy walk transitioning to light jog
- 8 leg swings/side (forward + lateral) + 10 ankle rolls/side + 10 calf raises

### Main Run (25–30 min easy)
- **4 km** at easy aerobic pace
  - Target pace: ~6:00–6:30 min/km (RPE 5–6, conversational, nasal breathing preferred)

  - If 4 km feels genuinely easy, extend to 5 km; if breathing labours, shorten to 3 km

### Cool-down (5 min)
- 3 min easy walk
- Wall calf stretch 2 × 45 s/side
- Standing hamstring reach 2 × 30 s/side

## Friday — Back Squat (Functional Fitness)

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 60 s easy row
  - 10 air squats (slow, 3 s pause at bottom)
  - 10 glute bridges + 8 side-lying clamshells/side
  - 8 leg swings/side + 6 world's greatest stretch/side (lunge + thoracic rotation)

### Strength (15 min)
- Back Squat 3×5 @ ~55%
  - Tempo: 3-1-1
  - Cues: bar low-bar or high-bar per preference, brace before descent, knees track toes

- 15 KB swings (16 kg, Russian)
- 8 strict chin-ups (scale: 6 reps or band-assisted)

- Core (5 min): 3 rounds × (15 hollow rocks + 10 hanging knee raises + 20 s plank)

- Couch stretch 2 × 45 s/side
- Seated single-leg hamstring fold 2 × 45 s/side
- Kneeling dorsiflexion rocks 2 × 12/side
