- **Post-workout (Mon / Wed / Fri):** 40–50 g protein + 60–70 g carbs within 45 min

### Recovery & Deficit Notes
- …
- **Ankle monitoring:** If HSPU wall-work volume is creeping above ~8 min on Mondays, flag it and shift more volume to Wednesday DB press sets rather than adding wall time.
- **Pull-up tracking:** Record load and reps for weighted sets each Monday. Horizontal row volume (Wed/Fri) should trend upward in either load or reps each week.
- **Adjustment rule:** If two consecutive FF sessions feel flat despite hitting 265 g carbs, raise to +30 g carbs on training days and monitor for one week.
- **Scale tracking:** ~0.5–0.75 kg/week fat loss expected. If loss > 1 kg/week, add +100–150 kcal via carbs immediately.
- **Hydration:** 3.5–4 L water daily baseline; add ~500 ml per training hour; electrolytes on rowing and running days.
