# Day 5 — Friday (2026-07-04)

## Warm-up (10 min)

- 2 rounds:
  - 10 glute bridges (2 s hold at top)
  - 8 air squats with 3 s pause at bottom
  - 8 inchworms (walk hands out to plank + walk back)
  - 10 scapula pull-ups on bar
- Ankle prep: kneeling dorsiflexion rocks 1 × 12/side

---

## Strength (15–20 min)

- **Back Squat 4 × 5 @ ~66% (~87.5 kg)**, rest 2:00
  - Cues: big breath and brace before descent, bar sits on rear delts (low bar) or traps (high bar — your preference), elbows point down under the bar, knees track toes through the full range, drive out of the hole
  - Note: reactivation week — every rep controlled; this should feel moderate; load steps up each week
- **Accessory — Chin-ups: 3 × 5 bodyweight**, rest 90 s
  - Cues: supinated grip, dead hang start, pull chin to bar, control the lower — no kip
  - Note: Week 1 baseline; record reps and any notes on shoulder/elbow feel; load adds from Week 3

---

## Metcon (AMRAP 18 — target 5–6 rounds)

- **12 box step-ups** @ 20 in (alternating legs, full hip extension at top) — sole cardio modality
- **8 hang power snatches** @ 30 kg
- **10 bar dips**
- **12 goblet squats** @ 20 kg

**Strategy:** Box step-ups at a consistent, unhurried pace — one breath per step; don't rush this movement. Hang power snatches unbroken all rounds (light load — this should be fast, snappy cycling); if form deteriorates, drop to 27.5 kg. Bar dips unbroken first 3 rounds then 6+4. Goblet squats unbroken throughout — keep elbows up and torso vertical.

**Loading note:** 30 kg on hang snatch is intentionally light — Week 1 is about re-establishing the movement pattern cleanly, not load. Same for goblet squat at 20 kg.

## Skill / Core (13 min)

**Pressing — ring push-ups + seated DB press, no wall (8 min):**
- **Ring push-ups: 3 × 10** (rings at ~knee height for moderate difficulty; raise rings toward waist if too hard)
  - Cues: keep rings turned out at bottom, turn in at top (false grip rotation), body rigid plank throughout
- **Seated DB press: 3 × 8 @ 10 kg/hand**, rest 60 s
  - Cues: seated upright, press straight up, elbows just in front of the body at start, full lockout at top
- Note: Third pressing session of the week (Mon wall, Wed seated DB, Fri ring) — keep quality over quantity; stop a set early if shoulder fatigue compromises form.

**Core (5 min):**
- 3 rounds:
  - 10 V-ups (full extension on the way down, controlled crunch to touch toes)
  - 25 s plank hold (forearm or straight arm — choose the harder option)
