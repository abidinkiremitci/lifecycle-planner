# Day 3 — Wednesday (2026-07-02)

## Warm-up (10 min)

- 2 rounds:
  - 8 hip 90/90 switches/side
  - 10 banded clamshells/side (medium band)
  - 8 KB Romanian deadlifts (16 kg, slow lower, feel hamstrings load)
  - 10 scapula push-ups (plus-minus protraction, straight arms)
- Hamstring activation: standing good morning × 10 (no load, exaggerated hip hinge)

---

## Strength (15–20 min)

- **Deadlift 4 × 5 @ ~67% (~110 kg)**, rest 2:00
  - Cues: hips back before the pull, neutral spine throughout, drive the floor away rather than pulling the bar, squeeze glutes hard at lockout
  - Note: reactivation week — brace before each rep, reset on the floor between reps (no touch-and-go), focus on crisp mechanics at this load; load steps up each week
- **Accessory — DB single-arm row: 3 × 10/side @ 22 kg**, rest 60 s between sides
  - Cues: flat back, elbow drives back and up, full range from hanging shoulder to hip pocket, 1 s hold at top
  - Note: Week 1 horizontal pull baseline — log this load; increase to 24–26 kg in Week 2 if the last 2 reps of each set feel controlled

---

## Metcon (AMRAP 18 — target 4–5 rounds)

- **10 burpees** — sole cardio modality
- **6 power cleans** @ 45 kg
- **10 push-ups**
- **12 KB swings** @ 24 kg (Russian — eye level)

**Strategy:** Burpees at a controlled, even cadence — not a sprint; establish a breathing rhythm in the first round and hold it. Power cleans unbroken through rounds 1–3 (touch-and-go or fast singles); break 3+3 from round 4 if grip is lagging. Push-ups unbroken first 3 rounds then 6+4. KB swings unbroken throughout — these should feel easy.

**Loading note:** 45 kg on power clean should be quick and upright — drop to 42.5 kg if the elbows are slow to turn over on the catch.

---

## Skill / Core (13 min)

**Pressing — DB overhead + pike push-ups, no wall (8 min):**
- **Seated DB overhead press: 3 × 8 @ 12 kg/hand**, rest 60 s
  - Cues: sit tall, brace core, press straight overhead, don't let the head jut forward at lockout
- **Pike push-ups from floor: 3 × 10**, slow lower (2 s descent)
  - Set-up: hips high, hands shoulder-width, lower crown toward floor between hands
- Note: Zero ankle contact today — these sets replace wall-work volume and build overhead pressing strength through a full ROM.

**Core (5 min):**
- 3 rounds:
  - 30 s side plank/side (add hip dip for load if easy)
  - 12 weighted sit-ups (8 kg plate at chest)
