## Warm-up (10 min)

- 2 rounds:
  - 8 cat-cow + thoracic rotations/side
  - 10 band pass-throughs + 8 scapula pull-ups on bar
  - 8 goblet squats (12 kg, 2 s pause at bottom)
  - 8 hip 90/90 switches/side
- Front-rack prep 2 min: wrist circles + elbows-high wall drill × 10 reps

## Strength (15–20 min)

- **Front Squat 4 × 5 @ ~67% (~70 kg)**, rest 2:00
  - Cues: elbows high and forward, upright torso, full-foot pressure, drive knees out, aggressive extension out of the hole
  - Note: reactivation week — all 5 reps crisp and controlled; do not grind; this load will step up each week
- **Accessory — Strict pull-ups: 3 × 5 bodyweight**, rest 90 s
  - Cues: dead hang start, full scapular depression before pulling, chin clearly over bar, controlled lower
  - Note: Week 1 baseline set — log reps per set; load adds from Week 3 onward

---

## Metcon (AMRAP 18 — target 5–6 rounds)

- **30 double unders** (alt: 60 single unders) — sole cardio modality
- **8 hang power cleans** @ 40 kg
- **10 bar dips**
- **12 AbMat sit-ups**

**Strategy:** Keep double unders at a relaxed, consistent rhythm — no sprinting the rope; 30 reps should finish in ~25–30 s. Hang power cleans unbroken through rounds 1–4 (quick reset at the hip); split 5+3 if grip fades in round 5. Bar dips unbroken first 3 rounds then 6+4 as needed. Sit-ups unbroken throughout.

**Loading note:** 40 kg on the hang clean should feel smooth and fast — if the first rep feels heavy, drop to 37.5 kg; this is a reactivation week, not a grind.

---

## Skill / Core (13 min)

**HSPU — wall work (8 min):**
- 3 rounds:
  - 20 s wall handstand hold (heels on wall, active shoulders, squeeze glutes, eyes between hands)
  - 3 controlled HSPU negatives (5 s descent, lower to AbMat or floor, kick down and reset — do not press up from the bottom yet)
  - Rest ~1:30 between rounds
- Note: This is the only session this week with ankle-on-wall contact. Cap total wall time at this volume — do not add extra sets even if the shoulders feel fresh.

**Core (5 min):**
- 3 rounds:
  - 10 dead bugs/side (slow, full extension, lower back stays flat)
  - 25 s hollow hold
