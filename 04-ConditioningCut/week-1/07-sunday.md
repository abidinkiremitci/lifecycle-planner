# Day 7 — Sunday (2026-07-06)

## Session: Running — Easy Base

---

## Warm-up (5 min)

- 3 min easy walk progressing to light jog
- Leg swings forward/back × 10/side
- Lateral leg swings × 10/side
- Ankle rolls × 10 each + calf raises × 15
- 2 × 20 m easy stride (build to 70% effort only)

---

## Main Run (28–32 min)

**5 km easy @ ~6:00/km**
- Target pace: 5:50–6:10/km
- RPE: 5–6 (Zone 2 — conversational, could hold a short sentence)
- Terrain: flat or gentle rolling preferred
- Indoor alternative: 25 min steady row @ 2:32–2:35/500 m (if running is not possible)

**Pacing guidance:**
- km 1: 6:05–6:10/km — hold back, do not start too fast coming off a full training week
- km 2–4: settle into 5:55–6:00/km as the legs warm up
- km 5: maintain current pace; no push at the end — this is a base-building run, not a time trial
- Decision rule: if legs feel heavy from the week's training, hold 6:10–6:15/km throughout and focus on duration over pace

**VO2 note:** Week 1 running establishes the aerobic base. Pace and distance build each week toward 10 km sub-50 min. Do not rush this progression.

---

## Cool-down (5 min)

- 4 min easy walk
- Wall calf stretch 2 × 45 s/side
- Standing quad stretch 45 s/side
- Seated single-leg hamstring fold 45 s/side
- Supine 90/90 diaphragmatic breathing 1 min
