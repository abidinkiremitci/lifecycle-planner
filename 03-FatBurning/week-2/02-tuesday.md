# Day 2 - Tuesday

## Indoor Rowing

### Warm-up (5 min)
- 4 min easy row (18 spm)
- 10 leg swings/side, 10 shoulder rolls/direction

### Main Row (28-34 min)
- 6 km steady row @ <=2:32/500 m
- Stroke rate: 19-21 spm
- Effort: Zone 2 to low Zone 3, RPE 6
- Pacing note: start first 2 km conservative and negative split the final 2 km

### Cool-down (5 min)
- 2-3 min easy row
- Hip flexor stretch 45 s/side
- Calf wall stretch 45 s/side
