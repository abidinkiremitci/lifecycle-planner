# Day 5 - Friday

## LIIT Fat-Burner (Early Morning, 15 min total)
- 3 rounds:
  - 2:00 easy rope jump (single-unders)
  - 2:00 easy row (16-18 spm)
  - 1:00 light kettlebell deadlift (16 kg)
- Rest: 20-30 s easy walk between rounds
- Target effort: RPE 4-5

---

## Main Functional Fitness - Back Squat

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 8 bodyweight squats with 2 s pause
  - 10 band lateral steps/side
  - 8 hip airplanes/side (supported)
  - 10 scap wall slides
- Then: ankle dorsiflexion rocks 10/side

### Strength (18 min)
- Back Squat 5 x 4 @ 64-66% (around 84-86 kg), rest 2:00
  - Cues: brace, knees track toes, fast concentric drive
- Accessory superset x 3:
  - Chin-ups x 6-8 (band-assisted if needed)
  - Ring dips x 8-10

### Metcon (EMOM 20)
- Min 1: 40 double-unders (or 70 single-unders)
- Min 2: 10 alternating dumbbell clean and jerks (2 x 15-17.5 kg)
- Min 3: 14 kettlebell swings (24 kg, Russian height)
- Min 4: 12 ab-mat sit-ups
- Min 5: Rest
- Cardio modality: jump rope only
- Target: each work minute finishes in 40-45 s; if rest drops below 10 s, reduce DU to 30-35 or singles to 55-60

### Skill / Core (12 min)
- HSPU rep-capacity progression (8 min):
  - 5 rounds: 3 pike HSPU + 1 controlled kipping HSPU + 15-20 s wall handstand hold
- Core (4 min):
  - 3 sets: 20 s hollow rock + 10 hanging knee raises

### Cool-down (8-10 min)
- Couch stretch 2 x 45 s/side
- Banded hamstring floss 45 s/side
- Thoracic extension over foam roller 8-10 reps
- Kneeling dorsiflexion rocks 2 x 12/side
- Easy nasal breathing 2 min
