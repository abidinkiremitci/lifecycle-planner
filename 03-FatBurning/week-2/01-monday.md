## Main Functional Fitness - Front Squat

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 8 cat-cow + thoracic rotations/side
  - 10 band pull-aparts + 10 scap push-ups
  - 8 goblet squats (light)
  - 8 bootstrappers
- Then: front-rack mobility flow 2 min (wrist + lat)

### Strength (18 min)
- Front Squat 5 x 4 @ 64-66% (around 60-62.5 kg), rest 2:00
  - Cues: elbows high, full-foot pressure, vertical torso
- Accessory superset x 3:
  - Ring rows x 12
  - Dips x 8-10 (band-assisted if needed)

### Metcon (For Time, cap 22)
- 5 rounds:
  - 14 box jump overs (24 in)
  - 8 hang power cleans (40 kg)
  - 12 front-rack reverse lunges (6/side, 40 kg)
  - 10 ab-mat sit-ups
- Cardio modality: box jump overs only
- Target finish: 18-22 min, keep transitions tight and breathing controlled

### Skill / Core (12 min)
- HSPU rep-capacity progression (8 min):
  - EMOM 8:
    - Odd: 3 controlled HSPU negatives + 10 s handstand hold
    - Even: 4 pike HSPU + 20 s handstand shoulder taps (wall-supported)
- Core (4 min):
  - EMOM 4: 35 s hollow hold + 25 s rest

### Cool-down (8-10 min)
- Couch stretch 2 x 45 s/side
- Seated single-leg hamstring fold 45 s/side
- Wall lat stretch (palm-up) 45 s/side
- Box breathing 4-2-6 x 2 min
