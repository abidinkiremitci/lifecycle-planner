# Nutrition - Week 2 (2026-05-18 to 2026-05-24)

## Weekly Focus
Week 2 is a build-conditioning week. Keep intake at cycle deficit baseline and do not under-fuel metcon days. Carbs should match training demand so conditioning volume can increase while strength quality remains stable.

## Macro Targets This Week

| Day Type | Days | Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Functional Fitness | Mon / Wed / Fri | ~3100 kcal | ~185 g | ~390 g | ~90 g |
| Endurance (Row / Run) | Tue / Sun | ~2900 kcal | ~185 g | ~340 g | ~90 g |
| Recovery LIIT (Optional) | Thu | ~2400 kcal (up to +150 if needed) | ~185 g | ~210 g | ~90 g |
| Rest / Active Recovery | Sat | ~2400 kcal | ~185 g | ~210 g | ~90 g |

## Key Timing Reminders
- Pre-LIIT fat-burner (early morning): fasted or minimal intake; 500 ml water on waking.
- Pre-main FF session (3-4 hours later): 400-600 kcal with 60-80 g carbs + 30-40 g protein, fat under 15 g.
- Intra-workout (if session exceeds 60 min): 30-40 g carbs/hour + 400-600 ml water/hour.
- Post-main FF session: 40-50 g protein + 60-80 g carbs within 45 min, keep fat low.

## Recovery & Fat-Loss Notes
- If two sessions in a row feel flat, add +25-30 g carbs on FF days before reducing training volume.
- Hydration baseline: 3.5-4.0 L/day + ~500 ml per training hour with electrolytes (500-700 mg sodium/L).
- If weekly loss exceeds 1 kg, add +100-150 kcal/day the following week.
- Track HSPU weekly rep totals and maintain controlled eccentrics to protect shoulders.
