# Day 3 - Wednesday (2026-05-27)

## LIIT Fat-Burner (Early Morning, 15 min total)
- 3 rounds:
  - 2:00 easy rope jump (single-unders)
  - 2:00 easy row (16-18 spm)
  - 1:00 mountain climbers (controlled pace, not explosive — keep hips level)
- Rest: 20-30 s easy walk between rounds
- Target effort: RPE 4-5

## Main Functional Fitness - Deadlift

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 10 glute bridges (1 s pause at top)
  - 8 inchworms with push-up
  - 10 banded good mornings
  - 8 scap pull-downs with band
- Barbell primer:
  - 5 Romanian deadlifts (60 kg)
  - 5 deadlifts (60 kg, controlled 3 s lowering)

### Strength (18 min)
- Deadlift 5 x 4 @ ~69% (~112.5 kg), rest 2:00-2:30
  - Cues: brace and lock before the pull, push the floor away, controlled 3 s descent, lock glutes hard at top
- Accessory superset x 3:
  - Strict pull-ups x 5-7 (add band only if needed to maintain strict form)
  - Deficit push-ups x 16-18 (hands on 2 × 10 kg plates)

### Metcon (AMRAP 24)
- 8 burpee box jump overs (24 in) — sole cardio modality
- 12 deadlifts (65 kg)
- 10 alternating KB snatches (24 kg)
- 10 ring dips (banded if needed to keep unbroken)
- Strategy: aim for consistent 3:30-4:00 rounds; protect the ring dips early — break 6+4 if needed from round 3.
- Target: 5-7 rounds

### Skill / Core (12 min)
- HSPU progression (8 min):
  - Every 2 min × 4 sets: max quality kipping HSPU to RPE 8 (target 6-8 reps per set) + 20 s freestanding HS balance attempt (or wall hold if needed)
- Core (4 min):
  - 3 rounds: 30 s side plank/side + 12 slow V-ups

### Cool-down (10 min)
- Couch stretch 2 x 45 s/side
- Prone quad stretch 45 s/side
- Pec doorway stretch 45 s/side
- Supine 90/90 diaphragmatic breathing 2 min
