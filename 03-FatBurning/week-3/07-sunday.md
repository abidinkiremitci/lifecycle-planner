# Day 7 - Sunday (2026-05-31)

## Running

### Warm-up (5 min)
- 3 min easy walk-jog
- 10 leg swings each direction/side
- 12 calf raises + 10 ankle rolls/side

### Main Run (~45-50 min)
- **Progressive 7 km run:**
  - km 1-2: easy pace ~6:15-6:30/km (RPE 4-5) — do not force pace, warm up the legs
  - km 3-5: build to comfortable tempo ~5:40-6:00/km (RPE 6-7) — find a sustainable rhythm
  - km 6-7: push toward goal tempo ~5:20-5:30/km (RPE 7-8) — if feeling good; if fatigued from toddler care, hold 5:45-6:00 and accept it
- Terrain: flat route preferred
- **Fatigue override:** If pre-run energy is very low (toddler night duty, poor sleep), reduce to an easy 5 km @ conversational pace — quality effort over forced distance.
- Indoor alternative (weather/time):
  - 40 min continuous easy row @ conversational pace (2:25-2:35/500 m, zone 1-2)

### Cool-down (5 min)
- 3 min easy walk
- Wall calf stretch 2 x 45 s/side
- Seated hamstring fold 45 s/side
- Hip flexor kneeling stretch 30 s/side
