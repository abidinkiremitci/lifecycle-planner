# Week 3 Nutrition — 03-FatBurning (2026-05-25 to 2026-05-31)

## Weekly Focus

Week 3 enters the **Push Metabolic Output** phase. Metcon duration and rep volume both increase (22-26 min target, 5-round sessions with devil press, burpee box jump overs, and double-unders). To protect metcon quality and prevent mid-session energy crashes, Functional Fitness day carbs are raised by +30 g above the deficit baseline. Protein remains fixed at 185 g to protect lean mass under the ongoing caloric deficit. If energy or performance drops notably mid-week — particularly on Wednesday — increase carbs by an additional +25-30 g on Thursday's recovery day as a buffer.

## Key Timing Reminders

- **Pre-LIIT fat-burner (early morning Mon/Wed/Fri):** Fasted or minimal intake; 500 ml water only before LIIT
- **Pre-main FF session (3-4 hours after LIIT):** 400-600 kcal with 60-80 g carbs + 30-40 g protein, fat <15 g
- **Post-main FF session (within 45 min):** 40-50 g protein + 60-80 g carbs, fat <10 g
- **Pre-Tuesday row (60-90 min before):** 400-500 kcal with 60-75 g carbs + 30 g protein — intervals demand adequate fuelling
- **Pre-Sunday run:** 300-400 kcal light carb-protein snack 60 min before; avoid heavy fat

## Recovery & Fat-Loss Notes

- **Phase adjustment:** +3% calories and +30 g carbs on FF days versus deficit baseline this week. This is intentional — high-rep metcons (devil press, burpee box jump overs) require fuel to sustain output quality.
- **Running note:** Running pace has been below target due to toddler fatigue. Prioritise sleep and carb intake on Saturday rest day to support Sunday run. Do not restrict Saturday carbs in an attempt to compensate — rest day fuelling directly impacts next-day performance.
- **Deficit reminder:** Target fat loss ~0.5-0.75 kg/week. If W2-to-W3 scale drop exceeds 1 kg, add +100-150 kcal to all training days starting W4.
- **HSPU volume focus:** W3 targets 6-8 kipping reps per set (up from 5). Total weekly HSPU volume should increase — track total reps across all three FF sessions.
- **Hydration:** 3.5-4 L water daily baseline; +500 ml per training hour. Add electrolytes (sodium 500-700 mg/L) on Tuesday intervals and any session with heavy sweating.
- **If Wednesday mid-week fatigue recurs** (like W2 after social event): bump Thursday recovery-day carbs to ~240-250 g and add a small carb top-up pre-Friday LIIT.
