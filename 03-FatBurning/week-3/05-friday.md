# Day 5 - Friday (2026-05-29)

## LIIT Fat-Burner (Early Morning, 15 min total)
- 3 rounds:
  - 2:00 easy rope jump (single-unders)
  - 2:00 easy row (16-18 spm)
  - 1:00 light KB swings (12-16 kg, soft Russian height — hip drive only, not aggressive)
- Rest: 20-30 s easy walk between rounds
- Target effort: RPE 4-5

---

## Main Functional Fitness - Back Squat

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 8 BW squats with 3 s pause at bottom
  - 10 band lateral steps/side
  - 8 single-leg RDL/side (bodyweight, focus on balance)
  - 10 thoracic spine rotations/side (seated on box)
- Ankle mobility: kneeling dorsiflexion rocks 10/side

### Strength (18 min)
- Back Squat 5 x 3 @ ~71% (~97.5 kg), rest 2:00-2:30
  - Cues: big breath brace before unracking, elbows driving up under bar, knees track toes, explosive drive out of the hole
- Accessory superset x 3:
  - Strict chin-ups x 7-9 (band-assisted only if needed to stay strict)
  - Dips x 8-10 (ring dips preferred)

### Metcon (EMOM 25)
- Min 1: 40 double-unders (or 80 single-unders) — sole cardio modality
  - DU note: only attempt 40 if you can finish in ≤35 s; otherwise cap at 30 DU or 80 SU to protect rest time
- Min 2: 12 KB swings (28-32 kg, American height)
- Min 3: 10 alternating dumbbell clean and jerk (2 × 17.5 kg)
- Min 4: 10 ring rows + 6 push-ups
- Min 5: Rest
- Target: each work minute completes in 40-45 s; if rest drops below 10 s on any minute, reduce reps by 1-2 next round

### Skill / Core (12 min)
- HSPU progression (8 min):
  - 4 rounds: 5-6 kipping HSPU + 3 HSPU negatives (5 s descent) + 15 s wall handstand hold
  - Aim to match or exceed Friday W2 volume (5 × 5 reps); push for a 6-rep set on rounds 2-3
- Core (4 min):
  - 3 rounds: 25 s hollow rock + 10 hanging knee raises (extend legs if possible for L-sit tuck)

### Cool-down (10 min)
- Couch stretch 2 x 45 s/side
- Banded hamstring floss 45 s/side
- Thoracic extension over foam roller 8-10 reps
- Kneeling dorsiflexion rocks 2 x 12/side
- Easy nasal breathing 2 min
