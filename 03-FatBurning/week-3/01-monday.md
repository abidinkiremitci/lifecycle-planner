## Main Functional Fitness - Front Squat

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 8 cat-cow + thoracic rotations/side
  - 10 band pull-aparts + 10 scap push-ups
  - 10 goblet squats (12 kg, 2 s pause at bottom)
  - 8 bootstrappers
- Front-rack mobility flow 2 min: wrist circles + elbow drive drill against wall

### Strength (18 min)
- Front Squat 5 x 3 @ ~71% (~70-72.5 kg), rest 2:00-2:15
  - Cues: elbows high and punching forward, full-foot pressure, vertical torso, explosive drive out of the hole
- Accessory superset x 3:
  - Ring rows (feet elevated on box) x 10
  - Ring push-ups x 10

### Metcon (For Time — cap 26 min)
- 5 rounds:
  - 10 devil press (2 × 20 kg DBs) — sole cardio modality
  - 8 hang power cleans (45 kg)
  - 10 front-rack reverse lunges (5/side, 45 kg)
  - 12 ab-mat sit-ups
- Strategy: settle into a pace you can hold all 5 rounds; don't redline on devil press in round 1. Break devil press 5+5 from round 3 onward if needed.
- Target: 22-26 min

### Skill / Core (12 min)
- HSPU progression (8 min):
  - EMOM 8:
    - Odd: 4 controlled HSPU negatives (5 s descent) + 10 s wall handstand hold
    - Even: max unbroken pike HSPU in :35 (target 8-10 reps)
- Core (4 min):
  - 4 rounds: 40 s hollow hold + 20 s rest

### Cool-down (10 min)
- Couch stretch 2 x 45 s/side
- Wrist flexor + extensor stretch 45 s each (devil press relief)
- Wall lat stretch (palm-up) 45 s/side
- Box breathing 4-2-6 x 2 min
