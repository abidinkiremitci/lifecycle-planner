# Day 2 - Tuesday (2026-05-26)

## Indoor Rowing

### Warm-up (5 min)
- 3 min easy row (18 spm, relaxed drive)
- 10 hip flexor openers/side
- 8 shoulder rolls each direction

### Main Row (~35 min)
- 5 × 1,000 m intervals
- Target split: ≤2:20/500 m per interval (note: your week 2 steady-state avg was 2:10 — use that as your ceiling and target negative splits across the 5 intervals)
- Stroke rate: 22-24 spm during intervals
- Rest between intervals: 2:30 easy paddle
- RPE: 7-8 per interval; aim for consistent pacing — resist the urge to sprint early intervals
- Pacing note: intervals 1-2 at ≤2:25, build to ≤2:20 on intervals 3-5

### Cool-down (5 min)
- 2 min easy row (16 spm)
- Seated single-leg hamstring fold 45 s/side
- Kneeling hip flexor stretch 45 s/side
