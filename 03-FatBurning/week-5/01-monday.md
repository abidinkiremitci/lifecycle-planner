## Main Functional Fitness — Front Squat

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 8 cat-cow + thoracic rotations/side
  - 10 band pull-aparts + 10 scap push-ups
  - 10 goblet squats (12 kg, 2 s pause at bottom)
  - 8 bootstrappers
- Front-rack mobility flow 2 min: wrist circles + elbow drive drill against wall

### Strength (18 min)
- Front Squat 4 × 3 @ ~76% (~80 kg), rest 2:00–2:15
  - Cues: elbows punching forward, vertical torso, full-foot pressure, explosive drive from the hole
  - Note: heavier than W3 (75 kg → 80 kg); lower sets vs W3 (4 vs 5) — every rep counts; zero misses
- Accessory superset × 3:
  - Strict pull-ups × 8–10
  - Ring push-ups × 12

### Metcon (AMRAP 24)
- 40 DU (alt: 80 SU) — sole cardio modality
- 8 push jerks (52.5 kg)
- 10 strict pull-ups (ring rows as scale)
- 12 KBS (24 kg)
- Target: 5–6 complete rounds
- Strategy: maintain a steady DU rhythm — break 20+20 if needed; push jerks unbroken first 3 rounds (break 5+3 from round 4 if overhead fatigue builds); pull-ups break 5+5; KB swings unbroken throughout
