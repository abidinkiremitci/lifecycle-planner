## Main Functional Fitness — Back Squat

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 8 BW squats with 3 s pause at bottom
  - 10 band lateral steps/side
  - 8 single-leg RDL/side (bodyweight, balance focus)
  - 10 thoracic rotations/side (seated on box)
- Ankle mobility: kneeling dorsiflexion rocks 10/side

### Strength (18 min)
- Back Squat 4 × 3 @ ~75% (~100 kg), rest 2:00–2:15
  - Cues: big breath brace before unracking, elbows driving up under bar, knees track toes, explosive drive out of the hole
  - Note: heavier than W4 (90 kg → 100 kg) — final push week; 3 reps means full intent on every single rep
- Accessory superset × 3:
  - Strict chin-ups × 8–10 (band-assisted only if needed to stay strict)
  - Ring dips × 10–12

### Metcon (AMRAP 22)
- 8 burpee box jump overs (24 in, step down — no rebounding) — sole cardio modality
- 6 power snatches (40 kg)
- 10 ring dips
- 12 KBS (24 kg)
- Target: 5–6 complete rounds
- Strategy: burpee box jump overs at a controlled, consistent pace — step down every rep; power snatches unbroken first 3 rounds (break 3+3 from round 4 if needed); ring dips break 5+5 from round 3; KB swings unbroken throughout

### Skill / Core (12 min)
- HSPU progression (8 min):
  - 4 rounds: 8 kipping HSPU + 3 HSPU negatives (5 s descent) + 20 s wall handstand hold
  - Aim to push total kipping HSPU volume beyond W4 Friday (>40 reps over 4 sets); attempt a set of 10+ on round 2 if shoulder freshness allows — W6 includes a max-rep HSPU test
- Core (4 min):
  - 3 rounds: 25 s hollow rock + 12 hanging knee raises (extend legs toward L-sit tuck where possible)

### Cool-down (10 min)
