# Day 2 — Tuesday (2026-06-09)

## Indoor Rowing — Intervals

### Warm-up (5 min)
- 3 min easy row (18 spm, relaxed drive)
- 10 hip flexor openers/side
- 8 shoulder rolls each direction

### Main Row (~35 min)
- 6 × 800 m, rest 2:00 between intervals
- Target split: ≤2:05/500 m per interval
- Stroke rate: 22–24 spm
- RPE: 7–8 per interval, easy walk/rest during recovery
- Pacing: first 2 intervals at ~2:07–2:08 to settle rhythm; build to ≤2:05 from interval 3 onward; aim for negative splits — last 2 intervals should be your fastest
- Note: W4 steady-state averaged 2:08/500 m for 7 km — these intervals push beyond that ceiling; the 2:00 rest keeps intensity high across all 6 efforts
- Total work distance: 4,800 m

### Cool-down (5 min)
- 2 min easy row (16 spm)
- Seated single-leg hamstring fold 45 s/side
- Kneeling hip flexor stretch 45 s/side
