  - 8 scap pull-downs with band

  - 5 Romanian deadlifts (60 kg)

### Strength (18 min)
- Deadlift 4 × 3 @ ~72% (~120 kg), rest 2:00–2:15

  - Note: heavier than W4 (110 kg → 120 kg) — final push week; move with aggression off the floor; zero misses
- Accessory superset × 3:
  - Strict pull-ups × 8–10 (add band only if needed to maintain strict form)
  - Deficit push-ups × 14–16 (hands on 2 × 10 kg plates)
