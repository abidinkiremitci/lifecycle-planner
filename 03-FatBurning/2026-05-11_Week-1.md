# Week 1 – Reactivation @ Deficit (2026-05-11 to 2026-05-17)

## Weekly Overview
- Cycle: 03-FatBurning
- Week: 1
- Dates: 2026-05-11 to 2026-05-17
- Load target: ~55-62%
- Metcon target: 15-18 min
- Focus: Re-establish training rhythm in deficit, keep lifting quality high, and accumulate clean conditioning volume without excessive fatigue.
- Days: Monday (LIIT + FF), Tuesday (Row), Wednesday (LIIT + FF), Thursday (Run), Friday (LIIT + FF)
- Session structure (Mon/Wed/Fri): LIIT fat-burner (early morning, 15 min) -> Main FF session (60-70 min) 3-4 hours later

## Notes
- First week in a fresh deficit: keep reps smooth, avoid missed lifts, and leave 1-2 reps in reserve on strength sets.
- One cardio movement per metcon only.
- No kipping pull-ups, toes-to-bar, or chest-to-bar.
- No bench press, strict press, or back squats in metcons.
- Space: 5 m x 4 m, so no long-distance walking prescriptions.
- If morning LIIT feels too draining, keep it strictly Zone 2 and reduce each round by 30-45 seconds.

## Nutrition

### Weekly Focus
Week 1 is a reactivation week under deficit. Keep intake consistent, prioritize training-day carbs around sessions, and avoid aggressive additional cuts. The target is to adapt to the new calorie level while preserving strength quality and stable recovery.

### Macro Targets This Week

| Day Type | Days | Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Functional Fitness | Mon / Wed / Fri | ~2945 kcal | ~185 g | ~365-370 g | ~90 g |
| Endurance (Row / Run) | Tue / Thu | ~2755 kcal | ~185 g | ~315-320 g | ~90 g |
| Rest / Active Recovery | Sat / Sun | ~2400 kcal | ~185 g | ~210 g | ~90 g |

### Key Timing Reminders
- Pre-LIIT fat-burner (early morning): fasted or minimal intake; 500 ml water on waking.
- Pre-main FF session (3-4 hours later): 400-600 kcal with 60-80 g carbs + 30-40 g protein, fat under 15 g.
- Intra-workout (if session exceeds 60 min): 30-40 g carbs/hour + 400-600 ml water/hour.
- Post-main FF session: 40-50 g protein + 60-80 g carbs within 45 min, keep fat low.

### Recovery & Fat-Loss Notes
- If two sessions in a row feel flat, add +25-30 g carbs on FF days before reducing training volume.
- Hydration baseline: 3.5-4.0 L/day + ~500 ml per training hour with electrolytes (500-700 mg sodium/L).
- HSPU volume focus: log weekly total reps and hold seconds; aim for small, consistent increases each week.
