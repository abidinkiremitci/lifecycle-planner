## Main Functional Fitness — Front Squat

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 8 cat-cow + thoracic rotations/side
  - 10 band pull-aparts + 10 scap push-ups
  - 10 goblet squats (12 kg, 2 s pause at bottom)
  - 8 bootstrappers
- Front-rack mobility flow 2 min: wrist circles + elbow drive drill against wall

### Strength (15 min)
- Front Squat 4 × 4 @ ~66% (~65 kg), rest 1:45–2:00
  - Cues: elbows high and punching forward, full-foot pressure, vertical torso, explosive drive out of the hole
  - Note: reduced volume vs W3 (4 sets vs 5) — focus on movement quality and speed out of the hole
- Accessory superset × 3:
  - Strict pull-ups × 6–8
  - Ring push-ups × 12

### Metcon (AMRAP 22)
- 10 box jump overs (24 in, step down — no rebounding) — sole cardio modality
- 8 hang power snatches (40 kg)
- 10 ring push-ups
- 12 weighted sit-ups (6 kg smash ball)
- Target: 5–6 complete rounds
- Strategy: keep the hang power snatches technically clean — break 5+3 from round 4 if needed; ring push-ups and sit-ups should stay unbroken through the first 3 rounds

### Skill / Core (12 min)
- HSPU progression (8 min):
  - EMOM 8:
    - Odd: 5 controlled HSPU negatives (5 s descent) + 10 s wall handstand hold
    - Even: max unbroken pike HSPU in :35 (target 9–11 reps — push 1–2 reps beyond W3)
- Core (4 min):
  - 4 rounds: 40 s hollow hold + 20 s rest

### Cool-down (10 min)

- Wrist flexor + extensor stretch 45 s each (front-rack / hang clean relief)
- Wall lat stretch (palm-up) 45 s/side
- Box breathing 4-2-6 × 2 min
