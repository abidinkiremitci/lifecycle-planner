# Day 7 — Sunday (2026-06-07)

## Running

### Warm-up (5 min)
- 3 min easy walk/jog
- 10 leg swings/side (front-to-back + lateral)
- 10 calf raises each foot

### Main Run (27–30 min)
- 5 km easy
- Target pace: ~6:30–7:00/km (or slower — pace is secondary to effort)
- RPE: 6–7, conversational breathing (you should be able to speak in full sentences)
- Pacing: start at whatever feels like a gentle shuffle; do not push; if breathing gets laboured, slow down
- Zone 2 base focus — run by feel, not by pace; 5:30/km is a long-term target, not today's standard

### Cool-down (5 min)
- 3 min easy walk
- Wall calf stretch 2 × 45 s/side
- Standing quad stretch 45 s/side
- Seated single-leg hamstring fold 45 s/side
