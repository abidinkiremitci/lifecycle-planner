# Week 4 Nutrition — 03-FatBurning (2026-06-01 to 2026-06-07)

## Weekly Focus

Week 4 is the **Sustain Pace** phase — mid-cycle management with a slight back-off on strength volume while maintaining conditioning intensity. After a partially completed Week 3 (2 functional fitness sessions + bike commute), the body arrives this week fresher than typical. Calorie and carb targets are marginally eased on Functional Fitness days (−2% calories, −15 g carbs from W3 levels) to align with reduced training load. Endurance and rest day targets remain at deficit baseline. Protein is fixed at 185 g throughout.

## Key Timing Reminders

- **Pre-LIIT fat-burner (early morning Mon/Wed/Fri):** Fasted or 500 ml water only before LIIT
- **Pre-main FF session (3–4 hours after LIIT):** 400–550 kcal with 55–75 g carbs + 30–40 g protein, fat <15 g
- **Post-main FF session (within 45 min):** 40–50 g protein + 60–80 g carbs, fat <10 g
- **Pre-Tuesday row (60–90 min before):** 400–500 kcal with 60–75 g carbs + 30 g protein — 7 km steady-state demands consistent fuelling even at reduced intensity vs W3 intervals
- **Pre-Sunday run:** 300–400 kcal light carb-protein snack 60 min before; avoid heavy fat

## Recovery & Fat-Loss Notes

- **Partial W3 adjustment:** Only 2 sessions + bike commute were completed in W3. Total caloric expenditure was below plan. If the scale shows no movement or a slight uptick, stay the course on W4 targets — do not restrict further; the deficit will re-establish naturally across a full training week.
- **Phase adjustment:** −2% calories and −15 g carbs on FF days from W3 levels. This is a planned mid-cycle relief to manage accumulated fatigue and maintain quality across Mon–Wed–Fri.
- **Hydration:** 3.5–4 L water daily baseline; +500 ml per training hour. Add electrolytes (sodium 500–700 mg/L) on Tuesday 7 km row and any heavy-sweat session.
- **HSPU volume tracking:** Continue tracking total reps per session. Target: maintain W3 output across each FF day despite reduced strength load.
- **If mid-week fatigue builds into Wednesday:** raise Thursday recovery-day carbs to ~240–250 g and add a small carb top-up before Friday LIIT.
- **W5 preview:** If W4 goes well and energy is solid by Friday, W5 ramps back to full deficit baseline on FF days with heavier loading. Ensure adequate sleep and Saturday rest fuelling to support this transition.
