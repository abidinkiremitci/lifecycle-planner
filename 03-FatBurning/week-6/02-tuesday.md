# Day 2 — Tuesday (2026-06-16)

## Indoor Rowing — Steady Benchmark Support

### Warm-up (6 min)
- 4 min easy row (18 spm, relaxed drive)
- 8 thoracic twists seated on erg
- 8 hip flexor rocks/side
- 2 × 15 s build pieces to 22 spm with 30 s easy between

### Main Row (~40 min)
- 8,000 m steady
- Target split: ≤2:32/500 m
- Stroke rate: 20–22 spm
- RPE: 6–7 — controlled, sustainable, no racing the monitor
- Pacing:
  - First 2 km: 2:34–2:35/500 m
  - Middle 4 km: settle into 2:30–2:32/500 m
  - Final 2 km: hold pace or negative split to 2:28–2:30/500 m if breathing stays calm
- Note: W5 intervals averaged 2:03/500 m, so this should feel comfortably submaximal — build aerobic confidence, not fatigue

### Cool-down (5 min)
- 2 min easy row (16 spm)
- Figure-four seated stretch 30 s/side
- Kneeling hip flexor stretch 45 s/side
- Wall calf stretch 45 s/side
