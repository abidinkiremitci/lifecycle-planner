## Main Functional Fitness - Front Squat

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 8 cat-cow + thoracic rotations/side
  - 10 band pull-aparts + 10 scap push-ups
  - 8 goblet squats (light)
  - 8 bootstrappers
- Then: front-rack mobility flow 2 min (wrist + lat)

### Strength (18 min)
- Front Squat 5 x 4 @ 60% (around 55-57 kg), rest 2:00
  - Cues: elbows high, full-foot pressure, vertical torso
- Accessory superset x 3:
  - Ring rows x 10-12
  - Dips x 6-8 (band-assisted if needed)

### Metcon (AMRAP 16)
- 8 burpees
- 10 hang power cleans (35 kg)
- 12 front-rack reverse lunges (6/side, 35 kg)
- Cardio modality: burpees only
- Target: 4-6 rounds, steady pacing

### Skill / Core (12 min)
- HSPU volume progression (8 min):
  - 4 rounds: 20 s wall-facing handstand hold + 4 pike push-ups
- Core (4 min):
  - EMOM 4: 30 s hollow hold + 30 s rest

### Cool-down (8-10 min)
- Couch stretch 2 x 45 s/side
- Seated single-leg hamstring fold 45 s/side
- Wall lat stretch (palm-up) 45 s/side
- Box breathing 4-2-6 x 2 min
