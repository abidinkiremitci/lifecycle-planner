# Day 3 - Wednesday

## LIIT Fat-Burner (Early Morning, 15 min total)
- 3 rounds:
  - 2:00 easy rope jump (single-unders)
  - 2:00 easy row (16-18 spm)
  - 1:00 dead bug (alternating, controlled)
- Rest: 20-30 s easy walk between rounds
- Target effort: RPE 4-5

---

## Main Functional Fitness - Deadlift

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 10 glute bridges
  - 8 inchworms
  - 10 banded good mornings
  - 10 scap pull-downs with band
- Then: barbell primer
  - 6 Romanian deadlifts (empty bar)
  - 6 deadlifts (empty bar)

### Strength (18 min)
- Deadlift 5 x 4 @ 60% (around 97-100 kg), rest 2:00
  - Cues: brace before pull, push floor away, controlled descent
- Accessory superset x 3:
  - Strict pull-ups or banded pull-ups x 5-8
  - Push-ups x 12-15

### Metcon (EMOM 16)
- Min 1: 12 single-unders + 8 dumbbell snatches alternating (15-20 kg)
- Min 2: 10 deadlifts (50 kg) + 6 ring rows
- Cardio modality: single-unders only
- Target: finish each minute in 40-45 s

### Skill / Core (12 min)
- HSPU volume progression (8 min):
  - Every 2 min x 4 sets: 3 controlled HSPU negatives + 20 s tripod hold
- Core (4 min):
  - 3 sets not for time: side plank 30 s/side + 10 slow V-ups

### Cool-down (8-10 min)
- Couch stretch 2 x 45 s/side
- Prone quad stretch 45 s/side
- Pec doorway stretch 45 s/side
- Supine 90/90 diaphragmatic breathing 2 min
