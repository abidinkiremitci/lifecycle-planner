# Day 5 - Friday

## LIIT Fat-Burner (Early Morning, 15 min total)
- 3 rounds:
  - 2:00 easy rope jump (single-unders)
  - 2:00 easy row (16-18 spm)
  - 1:00 light kettlebell deadlift (16 kg)
- Rest: 20-30 s easy walk between rounds
- Target effort: RPE 4-5

---

## Main Functional Fitness - Back Squat

### Warm-up (10 min)
- 2 rounds:
  - 8 bodyweight squats with 2 s pause
  - 10 band lateral steps/side
  - 8 hip airplanes/side (supported)
  - 10 scap wall slides
- Then: ankle dorsiflexion rocks 10/side

### Strength (18 min)
- Back Squat 5 x 4 @ 58-60% (around 78-80 kg), rest 2:00
  - Cues: brace, knees track toes, fast concentric drive
- Accessory superset x 3:
  - Chin-ups x 5-8 (band-assisted if needed)
  - Ring dips or bench dips x 8-10

### Metcon (For Time, cap 18)
- 4 rounds:
  - 16 box step-ups (24 in, alternating, bodyweight or light DBs 2 x 10 kg)
  - 10 power snatches (30 kg)
  - 12 sit-ups
- Cardio modality: box step-ups only
- Target finish: 14-18 min

### Skill / Core (12 min)
- HSPU volume progression (8 min):
  - 5 rounds: 3 pike HSPU reps + 15-20 s wall handstand hold
- Core (4 min):
  - 3 sets: 20 s hollow rock + 8 hanging knee raises

### Cool-down (8-10 min)
- Couch stretch 2 x 45 s/side
- Banded hamstring floss 45 s/side
- Thoracic extension over foam roller 8-10 reps
- Kneeling dorsiflexion rocks 2 x 12/side
- Easy nasal breathing 2 min

### Score

- Metcon: 10:03
