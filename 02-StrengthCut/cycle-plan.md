# 02-StrengthCut – Cycle Plan

## Overview

- **Focus:** Build strength off Cycle 1 test maxes while maintaining a moderate caloric deficit; execute the carry-over VO₂max rowing session from Week 4; continue HSPU skill progressions.
- **Duration:** 4 weeks (2026-04-14 to 2026-05-10)
- **Days per week:** 5
  - Mon / Wed / Fri: Functional fitness (strength + metcon + skill/core)
  - Tue: Indoor rowing (30–60 min, goal: 10 km under 50 min)
  - Thu: Running (30–60 min, goal: 10 km under 50 min)

### Goals

- Increase Front Squat to a new 5 RM of ≥85 kg (from 80 kg).
- Increase Deadlift to a new 5 RM of ≥147 kg (from 140 kg).
- Increase Back Squat to a new 5 RM of ≥120 kg (from 115 kg).
- Improve all three cycle benchmark times (Threshold, Engine, Finisher).
- Execute the carried-over VO₂max rowing session (Week 1 Tuesday).
- Progress toward freestanding HSPU: target a held wall-balance of 5+ s.
- **Rowing:** Build aerobic base toward 10 km under 50 min (target split ≤2:30/500 m).
- **Running:** Build running base; target 5 km in under 27 min (~5:20/km) by Week 4.

## Nutrition

### Cycle Nutrition Goals

- Enter a **moderate caloric deficit** (~8–9% below standard baseline) on all day types while keeping protein fixed at 185 g/day and fat at 90 g/day.
- Carbohydrates are the primary lever — reduced on rest and deload days; slightly elevated on push/heavy training days to protect strength performance.
- Do not cut calories so aggressively that strength progress stalls or recovery suffers. If two or more consecutive sessions feel flat or lifts regress, raise FF-day carbs by +30–40 g temporarily.
- Protein must remain at 185 g/day regardless of phase — this is the muscle-preservation anchor during the cut.
